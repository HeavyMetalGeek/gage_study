Fields§

§n_parts: usize

Number of parts: $p$

§n_operators: usize

Number of operators: $q$

§n_replicates: usize

Number of replicates: $n$

§dof_total: usize

Total degrees of freedom: $\nu_T = N - 1$, where $N$ is the number of measurements

§dof_parts: usize

Part degrees of freedom: $\nu_p = p - 1$, where $p$ is the number of parts

§dof_operators: usize

Operator degrees of freedom: $\nu_q = q - 1$, where $q$ is the number of operators

§dof_repeatability: usize

Repeatability degrees of freedom: $\nu_R = pq(n - 1)$, where $n$ is the number of replicates. If the part-operator term is neglected, $\nu_R = \nu_T - \nu_p - \nu_q$.

§dof_part_operator: usize

Part-operator interaction degrees of freedom: $\nu_{p \cdot R} = \nu_p * \nu_R$

§sumsq_total: f64

Sum of squared differences between every value and the grand mean $$ SS_T = \sum_{i=1}^{p} \sum_{j=1}^{q} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \left(x_{ijk} - \bar{x}_{…} \right)^2 $$

§sumsq_parts: f64

Sum of squared differences between each part mean and the grand mean $$ SS_p = qn \sum_{i=1}^{p} \left( \bar{x}_{i..} - \bar{x}_{…} \right)^2 $$

§sumsq_operators: f64

Sum of squared differences between each operator mean and the grand mean $$ SS_q = pn \sum_{j=1}^{q} \left( \bar{x}_{.j.} - \bar{x}_{…} \right)^2 $$

§sumsq_repeatability: f64

Sum of squared differences between each replicate and the replicate mean $$ SS_R = \sum_{i=1}^{p} \sum_{j=1}^{t} \sum_{k=1}^{q} \left(x_{ijk} - \bar{x}_{ij.} \right)^2 $$ If the part-operator term is neglected, $SS_R = SS_T - SS_p - SS_q$

§sumsq_part_operator: f64

Sum of squared differences for the part-operator interaction: $SS_R = SS_T - SS_p - SS_q$

§meansq_parts: f64

Mean of the squared differences for parts: $MS_p = \frac{SS_p}{\nu_p}$

§meansq_operators: f64

Mean of the squared differences for operators: $MS_q = \frac{SS_q}{\nu_q}$

§meansq_repeatability: f64

Mean of the squared differences for repeatability: $MS_R = \frac{SS_R}{\nu_R}$

§meansq_part_operator: f64

Mean of the squared differences for the part-operator interaction: $MS_{p \cdot R} = \frac{SS_{p \cdot R}}{\nu_{p \cdot R}}$

§f_parts: f64

F-statistic for parts: $F_p = \frac{MS_p}{MS_{p \cdot R}}$. If the part-operator term is negelected $F_p = \frac{MS_p}{MS_R}$.

§f_operators: f64

F-statistic for operators: $F_q = \frac{MS_q}{MS_{p \cdot R}}$. If the part-operator term is negelected $F_q = \frac{MS_q}{MS_R}$.

§f_part_operator: f64

F-statistic for the part-operator interaction: $F_q = \frac{MS_q}{MS_R}$

§use_interaction: bool

Flag for neglecting the part-operator interaction term
